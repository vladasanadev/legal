Privacy Policy – My App

Last updated: 31 October 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our service (“Service”).

2. How we use it

We use this data only to provide the Service — e.g. to let you schedule or post content to TikTok from your app.

3. Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with service providers we use to run the Service (hosting, logging) and when required by law.

4. Storage & security

We store data for as long as needed to provide the Service. We use reasonable security measures, but no method is 100% secure.

5. Third-party services (TikTok)

Your use of TikTok is also governed by TikTok’s own terms and policies. We do not control TikTok’s handling of your data.

6. Your choices

You can revoke our access to your TikTok account at any time in TikTok’s settings or by contacting us.
